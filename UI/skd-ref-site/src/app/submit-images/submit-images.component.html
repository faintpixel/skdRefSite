Thanks for thinking about submitting some images!

Currently we're only accepting people pictures (full body, heads, and hands).

General requirements:
  • Images must be yours to give. We can't accept images you just found online.
  • High resolution: Ideally at least 1080 pixels tall.
  • No street photography, unless you have a signed model release for it.
For full body pictures:
  • No cut off feet/heads/arms. Full body must be in frame.
  • All models must be aged 18+.
  • Nothing pornographic or overly sexualized.
  • Strong lighting is good, but the form must be fully visible (ie, no body parts fading into the background and becoming indistinguisable).
For heads:
  • Some smiling at the camera pictures are ok, but we'd really like diversity in the head angle and expression.

Wonderful! We'd love to hear from you.

Please email Artomizer at artomizer@sketchdaily.net

To increase chances of your photos being accepted, please include:
  • The type and quantity of images you'd like to submit
  • A link to the image set, or some sample images (if you need somewhere to upload them we can arrange this - please don't send a huge zip file)
  • Your name as you would wish it to be displayed on the site
  • A link to your website/social media that you would like displayed on the site (optional)
  • Any usage restrictions
  • Additional confirmation that these are your photos and not ones you came across online

Thank you, and looking forward to hearing from you!
